KEY FEATURES:

SALES PIPELINE MANAGEMENT:
  • Track deals by stage (Prospect → Closed)
  • Calculate probability-weighted forecast
  • Monitor win rate by rep and team
  • Identify deal velocity and bottlenecks

PROFITABILITY ANALYSIS:
  • Gross margin by product line (Solar, Boiler, Water)
  • Margin by customer segment
  • COGS tracking and analysis
  • Intercompany markup chain (WCI → Solartech → Hippos)

INVENTORY OPTIMIZATION:
  • Stock levels by product
  • Days Inventory Outstanding (DIO)
  • Reorder alerts when below minimum
  • Slow-moving SKU identification
  • Stock-out prevention

INTERCOMPANY RELATIONSHIPS:
  • WCI purchase tracking (cost of goods)
  • Hippos sales tracking (revenue channel)
  • Markup analysis at each stage
  • Sell-through rate monitoring

OPERATIONAL METRICS:
  • Monthly revenue vs. plan
  • Gross profit and margin trending
  • Customer count and concentration
  • Order fulfillment rates
  • Days sales outstanding

COLOR CODING IN EXCEL:

BLUE FONT = Input cells (change these)
  • Example: Probability assumptions, manual KPI entries, costs
  • These are meant to be updated monthly or for scenario analysis

BLACK FONT = Formula calculations (auto-update)
  • Example: Totals, averages, margins, forecasts
  • Don't edit these - they recalculate when inputs change
