COMMON QUESTIONS

Q: Where do I start?
A: Open README.md and read the "Getting Started" section (10 minutes)

Q: How long will setup take?
A: 10-15 hours total, spread over 4 weeks. Follow SETUP_CHECKLIST.md

Q: How long does it take per month after setup?
A: 2-4 hours per month for data entry and analysis

Q: What data do I need from Zoho?
A: Follow DATA_REQUIREMENTS.md - lists exact exports needed

Q: Can I customize the Excel file?
A: Yes! Add rows, change formulas, add sheets as needed. All documented.

Q: Can I link this to Zoho automatically?
A: Not yet. Use monthly CSV exports for now. Future: ask Zoho about API.

Q: The Excel file has a formula error - what do I do?
A: See README.md "Troubleshooting" section or INDEX.md "Troubleshooting"

Q: I have more than 10 jobs/20 customers - what do I do?
A: Copy rows as needed. See SETUP_CHECKLIST.md "Common Questions" section

MONTHLY WORKFLOW (After First Month)

Week 1: Export data from Zoho (1 hour)
  - CRM: Customers, Jobs, Leads
  - Inventory: Products, POs
  - Books: Invoices, Labor, Expenses

Week 2: Update Excel (2 hours)
  - Job Costing: Enter month's jobs
  - Customer Tracker: Update customer data
  - Verify all formulas calculate

Week 3: Analyze (1 hour)
  - Review Monthly KPIs dashboard
  - Compare to prior month
  - Identify variances

Week 4: Report (1 hour)
  - Present to leadership
  - Discuss insights
  - Plan next month

Total: 5 hours per month

EXCEL SHEETS AT A GLANCE

JOB COSTING
  What: Track every service job
  Inputs: Job ID, labor hours, rate, materials, revenue
  Outputs: Gross margin $, gross margin %
  How often: Daily/Weekly (entry), Monthly (summary)

CUSTOMER TRACKER
  What: Track lifetime value and repeat business
  Inputs: Customer ID, total jobs, total revenue, last service date
  Outputs: Repeat yes/no, days since service, repeat rate
  How often: Monthly (after job costing complete)

PRICING MASTER
  What: Product catalog and pricing strategy
  Inputs: Product name, COGS, selling price
  Outputs: Markup %, pricing assumptions
  How often: Quarterly or when prices change

PIPELINE & LEADS
  What: Sales opportunity tracking
  Inputs: Lead ID, value, probability, days in pipeline
  Outputs: Weighted value, pipeline metrics
  How often: Weekly (leads), Monthly (summary)

MONTHLY KPIs
  What: Master dashboard pulling all data
  Inputs: Assumptions (CAC, labor rate, overhead)
  Outputs: All key metrics automatically
  How often: Monthly (auto-updates when other sheets updated)
